 Turn Your Real Car Into a Brick-Built Masterpiece

BrickRide Customs creates custom building-block model kits based on customers' real-life vehicles. 
Whether you drive a sports car, classic muscle car, truck, daily driver, or fully customized show car, we transform it into a 
detailed collectible model designed specifically for you.

 About Us
At BrickRide Customs, we believe every vehicle tells a story. Our mission is to help car enthusiasts celebrate their rides by turning them into unique brick-built display models.
Each custom set is designed from customer-submitted photos and vehicle specifications to capture the details that make every car special.

 How It Works
 1. Submit Your Vehicle
Upload photos of your vehicle along with its year, make, model, color, and modifications.

 2. Custom Design
Our design team creates a digital brick model based on your vehicle.

 3. Approval Process
Review your custom design and request any changes before production.

 4. Production
We prepare the building instructions and collect the required pieces.

 5. Delivery
Receive your custom building kit and instructions directly at your door.
